# DSA 5e Combat Maneuvers Reference

This document contains comprehensive rules for combat maneuvers (Kampfsonderfertigkeiten) in DSA 5th Edition, sourced from the official Ulisses Regelwiki.

## Base Combat Rules

### Combat Values

#### Attacke (AT)

calculation: Full Combat Technique + 3 per MU point above 8

#### Parade (PA)

calculation: Half Combat Technique + 3 per primary attribute point above 8

#### Ausweichen (AW)

calculation: GE / 2

### Multiple Defenses

first defense penalty: No penalty

second defense penalty: -3

third defense penalty: -6

fourth defense penalty: -9

penalty reset: Penalties reset at the start of each new combat round

eligibility: Only heroes and characters with Fate Points can make multiple defenses per round

### Passierschlag (Opportunity Attack)

A free attack triggered by:
- Failed Flucht (flee) attempt
- Enemy critical success on defense
- Failed special maneuvers
- Moving through engagement distance without stopping

type: Free attack

AT penalty: -4

requires action: No

can defend: No

critical success: No

botch: No

combine with SF: No

per round limit: Unlimited

## Passive Combat Special Abilities

### Verteidigungshaltung (Full Defense)

type: Passive

PA bonus: +4

restriction: No actions this combat round

declaration: Must be declared at start of round

prerequisites: IN 13

techniques: All melee techniques

AP cost: 10

### Vorstoß (All-Out Attack)

type: Spezialmanöver

AT bonus: +2

restriction: No defenses this combat round

declaration: Must be declared at start of round

status restriction: Cannot use while Status: Liegend

prerequisites: GE 13

techniques: Dolche, Fächer, Fechtwaffen, Hiebwaffen, Kettenwaffen, Raufen, Schwerter, Stangenwaffen, Zweihandhiebwaffen, Zweihandschwerter

AP cost: 10

### Meisterparade (Masterful Parry)

type: Passive

effect: Multiple parades per KR get -2 penalty each instead of -3

prerequisites: GE 15 or KK 15

techniques: All melee techniques with Parade

AP cost: 25

### Beidhändiger Kampf I-II (Two-Weapon Fighting)

type: Passive

#### Base Penalty (no SF)

penalty: -2 AT/PA for both weapons

#### Stufe I

penalty: -1 AT/PA

prerequisites: GE 13

#### Stufe II

penalty: 0 AT/PA

prerequisites: GE 15 + Stufe I

wrong hand penalty: -4 AT/PA (removed by Vorteil: Beidhändig)

shield exception: PA penalty doesn't apply when defending with shield

techniques: Dolche, Fächer, Fechtwaffen, Hiebwaffen, Raufen, Schilde, Schwerter

AP cost: 20/35

**Two-Weapon Fighting Base Rules:**

- cannot use: Two-handed or chain weapons (except with shield)
- attack action: Both weapons attack with 1 action, separate AT rolls
- targeting: Can target different enemies in engagement distance
- basismanöver only: Only Basismanöver can be combined with attacks
- patzer effect: Patzer on first attack = no second attack
- defense: Defender uses separate defenses against each attack

## Basismanöver (Basic Maneuvers)

### Finte I-III (Feint)

type: Basismanöver

#### Stufe I

AT penalty: -1

defender PA penalty: -2

prerequisites: GE 13

AP cost: 15

#### Stufe II

AT penalty: -2

defender PA penalty: -4

prerequisites: GE 15 + Finte I

AP cost: 20

#### Stufe III

AT penalty: -3

defender PA penalty: -6

prerequisites: GE 17 + Finte II

AP cost: 25

techniques: Dolche, Fächer, Fechtwaffen, Hiebwaffen, Peitschen, Raufen, Schwerter, Stangenwaffen, Zweihandhiebwaffen, Zweihandschwerter

### Wuchtschlag I-III (Power Attack)

type: Basismanöver

#### Stufe I

AT penalty: -2

TP bonus: +2

prerequisites: KK 13

AP cost: 15

#### Stufe II

AT penalty: -4

TP bonus: +4

prerequisites: KK 15 + Wuchtschlag I

AP cost: 20

#### Stufe III

AT penalty: -6

TP bonus: +6

prerequisites: KK 17 + Wuchtschlag II

AP cost: 25

techniques: Hiebwaffen, Kettenwaffen, Raufen, Schwerter, Stangenwaffen, Zweihandhiebwaffen, Zweihandschwerter

## Passive Combat Special Abilities (Additional)

### Kampfreflexe I-III (Combat Reflexes)

type: Passive

#### Stufe I

effect: +1 INI

prerequisites: IN 13

AP cost: 10

#### Stufe II

effect: +1 INI (total +2)

prerequisites: IN 15 + Kampfreflexe I

AP cost: 15

#### Stufe III

effect: +1 INI (total +3)

prerequisites: IN 17 + Kampfreflexe II

AP cost: 20

techniques: All

### Verbessertes Ausweichen I-III (Improved Dodge)

type: Passive

#### Stufe I

effect: +1 Ausweichen

restriction: No BE modifiers or additional penalties to GS/INI

prerequisites: Körperbeherrschung 4

AP cost: 15

#### Stufe II

effect: +1 Ausweichen (total +2)

prerequisites: Körperbeherrschung 8 + Verbessertes Ausweichen I

AP cost: 20

#### Stufe III

effect: +1 Ausweichen (total +3)

prerequisites: Körperbeherrschung 12 + Verbessertes Ausweichen II

AP cost: 25

techniques: All

### Meisterliches Ausweichen (Masterful Dodge)

type: Passive

effect: Multiple dodges per KR get -2 penalty each instead of -3

prerequisites: IN 13, Verbessertes Ausweichen II

techniques: All (no technique requirement)

AP cost: 15

### Klingentänzer (Blade Dancer)

type: Passive

effect: Roll +1W6 for initiative (total 2W6)

restriction: No BE modifiers or additional penalties to GS/INI

prerequisites: IN 17, GE 15, Feindgespür, Kampfreflexe I

techniques: All

AP cost: 40

### Klingenfänger (Blade Catcher)

type: Passive

effect: +1 PA bonus for wielded parrying weapon

prerequisites: GE 13

techniques: All parrying weapons

AP cost: 10

### Schnellziehen (Quick Draw)

type: Passive

effect: Draw weapon as free action instead of 1 action

restriction: Requires appropriate sheathe/holster

prerequisites: FF 13

techniques: Dolche, Fächer, Fechtwaffen, Hiebwaffen, Kettenwaffen, Peitschen, Schwerter, Stangenwaffen, Zweihandhiebwaffen, Zweihandschwerter

AP cost: 10

### Blindkampf I-II (Blind Fighting)

type: Passive

effect: Reduces visibility modifier penalties by 1 step per level in melee

restriction: Does not affect ranged combat or other tests

#### Stufe I

prerequisites: IN 15, Sinnesschärfe 8

AP cost: 10

#### Stufe II

prerequisites: IN 17, Sinnesschärfe 12 + Blindkampf I

AP cost: 20

techniques: All melee techniques

### Geschossabwehr I-III (Projectile Defense)

type: Passive

effect: Defend against thrown weapons with Raufen-PA instead of Ausweichen

#### Stufe I

improvised weapons modifier: +0

other thrown weapons modifier: -2

spears/discus modifier: -4

thrown melee weapons modifier: -6

prerequisites: FF 13

AP cost: 5

#### Stufe II

modifier bonus: +2 per level after first

prerequisites: FF 15 + Geschossabwehr I

AP cost: 10

#### Stufe III

modifier bonus: +2 per level after first (total +4)

prerequisites: FF 17 + Geschossabwehr II

AP cost: 15

restriction: Does not work against bow/crossbow ammunition; requires free hand

techniques: Raufen only

## Spezialmanöver (Special Maneuvers)

### Sturmangriff (Charge Attack)

type: Spezialmanöver

AT penalty: -2

TP bonus: +2 + half GS (max +10 total)

requirements: 4 step running distance, GS 4+

failure effect: Defender gets Passierschlag

restriction: Cannot combine with Finte

prerequisites: MU 13, Vorstoß, Wuchtschlag I

techniques: Hiebwaffen, Raufen, Schwerter, Stangenwaffen, Zweihandhiebwaffen, Zweihandschwerter

AP cost: 25

### Gegenhalten (Counter-Attack Defense)

type: Spezialmanöver

effect: Instead of PA roll, roll AT. Success = parried. Failure = enemy deals +2 TP

side effect: Gain 1 Stufe Betäubung after each use

defense count: Counts as defense for cumulative penalty

restriction: Cannot use with Ausweichen

prerequisites: MU 15, Wuchtschlag II

techniques: All melee with PA value

AP cost: 20

### Riposte

type: Spezialmanöver

PA penalty: -2

effect: After successful parry, immediate Passierschlag against attacker

declaration: Must declare before enemy AT roll

restriction: No more defenses this KR after Riposte

basismanöver: Cannot combine with Basismanöver

prerequisites: GE 15

techniques: Dolche, Fächer, Fechtwaffen

AP cost: 20

### Ausfall I-II (Flurry Attack)

type: Spezialmanöver

initial AT penalty: -2

#### Stufe I

extra attacks: 1

first extra AT: -4 AT, -1 TP

prerequisites: Finte I

AP cost: 25

#### Stufe II

extra attacks: 2

first extra AT: -4 AT, -1 TP

second extra AT: -10 AT, -2 TP

prerequisites: Finte II

AP cost: 35

minimum TP: 1 per attack

target restriction: All attacks must target same enemy

basismanöver restriction: Basismanöver only applies to single AT

techniques: Dolche, Fächer, Fechtwaffen

### Entwaffnen (Disarm)

type: Spezialmanöver

#### One-Handed Weapons

AT penalty: -4

#### Two-Handed Weapons

AT penalty: -6

damage: 1W3 TP

effect: Target drops weapon on failed defense

restriction: Cannot target shields

prerequisites: GE 15

techniques: Fächer, Fechtwaffen, Hiebwaffen, Peitschen, Raufen, Schwerter, Stangenwaffen, Zweihandhiebwaffen, Zweihandschwerter

AP cost: 40

### Zu Fall bringen (Trip)

type: Spezialmanöver

#### Same Size or Smaller Target

AT penalty: -4

#### One Size Larger Target

AT penalty: -12

damage: 1W3 TP

effect: Target gains Status: Liegend on failed defense

size restriction: Cannot use vs 2+ size categories larger

target restriction: Only vs bipedal humanoids

prerequisites: KK 13

techniques: Peitschen, Stangenwaffen

AP cost: 20

### Umrennen (Knockdown)

type: Spezialmanöver

AT penalty: -2

damage: TP/2

effect: Defender rolls Körperbeherrschung (Kampfmanöver) vs TP dealt or gains Status: Liegend + 1 Stufe Betäubung

betäubung duration: Until end of next KR

prerequisites: None

techniques: Raufen only

AP cost: 8

### Haltegriff (Grapple)

type: Spezialmanöver

AT penalty: 0

effect: Target gains Status: Fixiert + Eingeengt

escape: Comparative Kraftakt (Ziehen & Zerren) probe

restriction: Attacker cannot defend while holding

requirement: Both hands free required

size restriction: Same/smaller size only

prerequisites: None

techniques: Raufen only

AP cost: 5

## Quick Reference: Common Maneuver Combinations

### Offensive Options

#### High AT, want more damage

recommended maneuver: Wuchtschlag

#### Enemy has high PA

recommended maneuver: Finte

#### Have running start

recommended maneuver: Sturmangriff

#### Enemy disarmed/barehanded

recommended maneuver: Entwaffnen

#### Multiple attacks needed

recommended maneuver: Ausfall

#### Knock enemy prone

recommended maneuver: Umrennen or Zu Fall bringen

#### No defense needed

recommended maneuver: Vorstoß

### Defensive Options

#### Multiple enemies

recommended maneuver: Verteidigungshaltung

#### Counter-attack wanted

recommended maneuver: Riposte or Gegenhalten

#### Multiple parades needed

recommended maneuver: Meisterparade

#### Grappling enemy

recommended maneuver: Haltegriff
