# Additional Combat Maneuvers (Missing from Reference)

## Spezialmanöver

### Doppelangriff (Double Attack)

- type: Spezialmanöver
- at penalty: -2 first AT, +2 second AT
- effect: Two attacks in one action
- prerequisites: Beidhändiger Kampf, IN 13, KK 13
- techniques: Any one-handed weapon
- ap cost: 25

### Todesstoß (Death Thrust)

- type: Spezialmanöver
- at penalty: -2
- effect: +1W6 damage on hit; on failed AT, enemy gets Passierschlag (free attack)
- prerequisites: Präziser Stich III, MU 15
- techniques: Thrusting weapons only
- ap cost: 30

### Hammerschlag (Hammer Blow)

- type: Spezialmanöver
- at penalty: -2
- effect: +1W6 damage on hit; on failed AT, enemy gets Passierschlag (free attack)
- prerequisites: Wuchtschlag III, MU 15
- techniques: Heavy weapons (Zweihand)
- ap cost: 25

### Wirbelangriff I-II (Whirlwind Attack)

- type: Spezialmanöver

#### Stufe I

- at penalty: -2 (2 enemies), -4 (3 enemies)
- tp penalty: -1
- effect: Attack 2-3 adjacent enemies simultaneously
- prerequisites: GE 15
- techniques: Light/medium weapons
- ap cost: 20

#### Stufe II

- at penalty: -4 (2 enemies), -10 (3 enemies)
- tp penalty: -2
- effect: Attack 2-3 adjacent enemies simultaneously
- prerequisites: GE 17
- techniques: Light/medium weapons
- ap cost: 30

### Rundumschlag I-II (Sweeping Strike)

- type: Spezialmanöver

#### Stufe I

- at penalty: -2 (2 enemies), -4 (3 enemies)
- tp penalty: -1
- effect: Attack 2-3 adjacent enemies simultaneously
- prerequisites: None
- techniques: Heavy two-handed weapons only
- ap cost: 25

#### Stufe II

- at penalty: -4 (2 enemies), -10 (3 enemies)
- tp penalty: -2
- effect: Attack 2-3 adjacent enemies simultaneously
- prerequisites: None
- techniques: Heavy two-handed weapons only
- ap cost: 35

### Betäubungsschlag (Stunning Blow)

- type: Spezialmanöver
- at penalty: -2
- effect: 1W3 TP + target must roll KO×3 or gain 1 Stufe Betäubung
- prerequisites: KK 13
- techniques: Raufen, heavy weapons
- ap cost: 15

### Gezielter Angriff (Targeted Attack)

- type: Spezialmanöver (requires Fokusregel: Trefferzonen)
- at penalty: See Trefferzonen table below
- effect: Target specific body locations for special effects
- prerequisites: GE 13
- techniques: All
- ap cost: 10

#### Trefferzonen AT Penalties (Humanoid, small to large)

- kopf (head): -10
- torso: -4
- arme (arms): -8
- beine (legs): -8

#### Modifiers

- target with status überrascht: Penalty reduced by 2
- with gezielter angriff sf: Penalties halved (rounded up)
- note: These penalties replace size category modifiers

## Ranged Combat Maneuvers

### Ballistischer Schuss (Ballistic Shot)

- type: Spezialmanöver
- effect: Ignore cover penalties, target loses RS from cover
- prerequisites: FF 15, Scharfschütze
- techniques: Bows, Crossbows
- ap cost: 25

### Präziser Schuss I-II (Precise Shot)

- type: Basismanöver

#### Stufe I

- at penalty: -2
- effect: Ignore cover RS bonus partially
- prerequisites: FF 13
- techniques: Bows, Crossbows, Thrown weapons
- ap cost: 15

#### Stufe II

- at penalty: -4
- effect: Ignore cover RS bonus partially
- prerequisites: FF 15 + PS I
- techniques: Bows, Crossbows, Thrown weapons
- ap cost: 25

### Scharfschütze (Sharpshooter)

- type: Passive
- effect: Reduce range penalty by 1 step
- prerequisites: FF 15, Sinnesschärfe 8
- techniques: Bows, Crossbows
- ap cost: 20

## Defensive Maneuvers

### Beschützer (Protector)

- type: Passive
- effect: Can intercept attacks targeting adjacent allies
- prerequisites: MU 13
- techniques: All melee
- ap cost: 20

### Eiserner Wille (Iron Will)

- type: Passive
- effect: Ignore 1 Stufe Betäubung per KR for defense calculations
- prerequisites: MU 15
- techniques: All
- ap cost: 15
